# BTCMind Decision Log

This log captures BTCMind product, agent, content, targeting, scheduling, and workflow decisions as they are made. During the experimentation stage, it also records important research prompts and framing questions that shape later decisions.

## 2026-05-31

### Question

What is BTCMind, what industry does it compete in, who are its competitors, and what are its upstream and downstream business relationships?

### Decision

Use @aixbt_agent as the primary X-native growth benchmark and @nansen_ai as the secondary product-trust benchmark for BTCMind X management. Emulate the operating mechanics, not risky token-call behavior.

### Decision

Adopt a Nansen-derived proof-distribution architecture for BTCMind:

signal source -> original post -> continuation reply -> community proof -> repost/quote -> analytics learning.

Build order:

1. Signal Source + Poster.
2. UGC/community radar.
3. Quote scout.
4. Continuation reply generator.
5. Reposter.
6. Analytics learner.

Reason: the Nansen benchmark showed that reposting works only when there is an external proof supply. BTCMind should first create postable product/market signals, then build the community loop that gives the repost autopilot useful material.
