# Feedback

Source date: 2026-06-17

User feedback captured for this article:
- Translate Chinese drafts into English before publishing.
- Do not publish a text-only article; include images.
- Use at least three images.
- Images must be informative, not just decorative.
- Avoid large paragraph gaps in X. Do not paste body text as many separate Draft.js paragraphs.
- Also avoid collapsing everything into one giant soft-break block; the next article showed that this can remove visible paragraph spacing.

Applied fixes:
- Article was translated into English.
- Three informative diagrams were added.
- Template copy behavior was changed to balanced paragraph grouping.

Carry forward:
- Future articles should start in English, include informative images, and use balanced X Article spacing: short related blocks can be grouped with soft line breaks, while headings, long blocks, and image positions stay visibly separated.
