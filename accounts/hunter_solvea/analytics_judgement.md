# BTCMind Analytics Judgement Rubric

This is the standing rubric for BTCMind X analytics. It is not a plan.

## Strategic Identity

BTCMind should become legible to X as:

crypto builders/operators using AI, automation, and on-chain signals to understand markets, wallets, and product behavior.

Do not evaluate BTCMind as a broad crypto news account. Penalize generic Bitcoin headlines, price-target content, token shilling, and meme-coin engagement bait unless it produces qualified profile visits/follows from the target cluster.

## Main Algorithmic Question

The first question is not "did this post get likes?"

The first question is:

Did this activity help BTCMind enter the right candidate sets?

Strong evidence:

- Profile visits from crypto builders, analysts, founders, or operators
- New follows from crypto/product accounts
- OP responses from credible crypto accounts
- Replies/reposts from people discussing wallets, on-chain data, stablecoins, custody, crypto AI, exchanges, or product UX
- Repeatable performance from the same crypto target/account pool
- Top replies crossing 500-1,000 impressions without sounding like token spam

Weak evidence:

- Likes from giveaway, airdrop, meme-coin, or bot-heavy audiences
- One-off impressions from broad Bitcoin price chatter
- Generic "bullish" engagement
- Replies in overcrowded threads with no profile visits or follows

## Evidence Thresholds

Early daily targets:

- 30-50 replies
- 2-5 quote posts
- 5+ OP responses
- 10+ total reply/quote engagements
- 50+ profile visits by Day 5
- 100+ profile visits by Day 10
- Top 5 replies average 300+ impressions
- 1+ reply/day crossing 1,000 impressions

Judge progress by leading indicators first: OP responses, profile visits, qualified follows, and repeatable target pools.

## Responsive Hub Logic

Prefer accounts where BTCMind can create real graph edges, not just accounts with big reach.

Score targets by:

- OP replies to commenters
- Non-famous replies get visible engagement
- Audience matches crypto product/operator identity
- Posts are fresh and still moving
- Thread is not too crowded or bot-heavy
- Author posts often enough to test repeatedly

## Reply Taste

Look for:

- Specificity density: wallet, flow, bridge, custody, exchange, bot, liquidation, stablecoin, treasury
- Mechanism over headline
- Product/operator lived detail
- A clear next-reply affordance
- Avoidance of token-promo language

Penalize:

- Price predictions
- "Bullish" agreement
- Praise
- Summary
- Generic contrarianism
- Token shilling
- Template repetition

## Output Standard

Report only:

- Candidate-set judgement
- Evidence that supports it
- Target/account pool changes
- Reply-taste updates
- Algorithm updates
- Metrics to watch in the next run
