## How to reply

1. **Lead with a data point** — open with a specific number the person hasn't mentioned
2. **Connect to the problem** — acknowledge what they're experiencing before introducing Solvea
3. **Mention Solvea only when relevant** — if the post is about missed calls, staffing cost, or phone handling, it's relevant. If it's general AI news, add value without the product pitch.
4. **One mention max** — never name Solvea twice in one reply
5. **Never hard sell** — "solvea handles this" is fine; "sign up at solvea.cx" is not

## What NOT to reply to
- Posts that are purely personal (travel, food, personal news)
- Political or controversial topics
- Posts from direct competitors — don't engage
- Posts already flooded with sales replies
